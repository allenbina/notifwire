notifwire

Self-hosted, peer-to-peer notification mesh for every device you own. Your notifications, your devices, your rules.

View on GitHub Why notifwire

What it does

📡 Captures everywhere

Native notifications from macOS, Windows, Linux, and Android — and iOS via Continuity.

🕸️ No central server

Every install is a node. Any always-on node is the hub. You own the whole pipeline.

🔀 Routes anywhere

Native delivery, plus HTTP webhook, MQTT, and Apprise's 100+ services.

🔒 Private

TLS everywhere, opt-in end-to-end encryption. No cloud, no third parties.

Free your phone-only alerts

2FA codes, app-only push, bank and delivery alerts — the notifications that today only ever reach your phone. notifwire delivers them to whatever device you're actually using.

Built for people who want control

notifwire is open source and self-hosted. No accounts, no telemetry, no lock-in. Currently in design stage — star the repo to follow along.
